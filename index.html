My Strength is...

My biggest strength is that I work as a team player. Being a team player requires more than just collaborative skills. A team player is someone who combines their personal abilities with teamwork skills to complete tasks or move ahead with projects in a more effective way. In other words, a team player is goal-oriented. I always understand my role and my abilities when I am in a team. Additionally, I will devote myself to the team and strive for the best for our team’s common goal, as well as help one another in the team with all my abilities when it is needed. When I was in high school, I was the one in class who initiated to form a study group. I became the bridge between my classmates who we helped each other in the subject we were good at. Finally, the university acceptance rates in our class were the highest, compared to other classes and the ones in the past. There is no you and me in a team as long as we are striving for the same goal.

My strength is conspicuously applicable to both summative tasks and informative tasks. If my classmates are aiming at getting better grades and improving themselves, then we could share our knowledge and help each other with our biggest abilities whenever there are tests or courseworks. More importantly, we support each other. I genuinely believe by doing so we all could have a huge improvement by the end of the term as both tasks require us to demonstrate our personal abilities and collobration skills in a certain way. Being a team player could catalyse the collaboration between teammates, thereby maximizing the effectiveness and efficiency of achieving our common goal - getting better grades.
